Sensitivity

Leave-one-subgroup-out: each cell is how much a property's apparent effect size changes when one subgroup is excluded from the comparison. Positive shrinkage means the effect got weaker without that subgroup -- that subgroup was driving it. Negative shrinkage means the effect got stronger without it -- that subgroup had been diluting it.
